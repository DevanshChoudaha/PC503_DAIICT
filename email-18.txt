It was in the fourth wave when the focus shifted to higher education and private participation. One of the Institutes created during this period is DA-IICT in 2001. 
The architecture of DA-IICT is functional, but what surrounds it is a fascinating garden.  
The Act No. 6 of 2003 of the Gujarat Legislature provided for the establishment of the DA-IICT and conferred on it the status of a University.
